# NFT Evolve

Dynamic NFT Evolution Engine - Digital pets that grow and evolve over time.

## Overview

NFT Evolve brings virtual pets to Neo N3. Mint a pet, care for it daily, and watch it evolve through 6 stages from Egg to Dragon.

## Features

- **Dynamic Evolution**: 6 evolution stages
- **Care Mechanics**: Feed and play to keep pet healthy
- **World Tick**: Automated stat decay over time
- **XP System**: Earn XP through interactions
- **Persistent State**: Pet survives across sessions
